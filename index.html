About Our Planet 🌍

The Earth is changing due to pollution caused by plastic waste, industrial emissions, and other harmful materials. Oceans are particularly affected, with millions of tons of plastic entering them every year, harming marine life and ecosystems.

What Pollutes Our Oceans? 🌊

Common pollutants include:

  • Plastic bags and bottles
  • Fishing gear 🎣
  • Chemicals from industrial runoff
  • Sewage and agricultural waste 🚯

How Can We Save Our Planet? 🌱

We can all contribute to saving our planet by:

  • Reducing plastic use and opting for reusable products.
  • Participating in local clean-up events.
  • Educating others about the importance of keeping our oceans clean.
  • Supporting legislation that protects marine environments.

How This Game Helps 🕹️

This game not only provides fun and engagement but also raises awareness about ocean pollution and encourages players to take action. By collecting trash in the game, you are reminded of the importance of keeping our oceans clean and the impact of pollution on marine life.
